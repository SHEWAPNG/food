Spaghetti Jollof with Egg

A spicy and delicious jollof spaghetti served with boiled eggs.

Ingredients:
  • 250g spaghetti
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2-4 boiled eggs (for garnish)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
Procedure:

1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente, drain, and set aside.

2. In a pan, heat oil, saut√© onions, and bell peppers. Add tomatoes and spices, cooking until thickened.

3. Combine the sauce with broth, then mix with the spaghetti.

4. Serve with boiled eggs and parsley garnish. Enjoy!
