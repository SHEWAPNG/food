Noodles with Fried Omelette

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of instant noodles (any flavor)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 small green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Boil 2 cups of water in a pot. Add the noodles and cook for 3-5 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Fried Omelette:
    • Whisk the eggs with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
    • Heat oil in a pan, saut√© the onion, tomato, and bell pepper for 2-3 minutes.
    • Pour the eggs over the vegetables and cook until golden on both sides. Slice the omelette.
  3. Serve: Place the noodles on a plate, top with the omelette slices, and enjoy!
